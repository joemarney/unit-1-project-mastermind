YOUR GOAL IS TO GUESS THE SECRET CODE WITHIN 8 TURNS.
FOR EACH TURN, SELECT A COMBO OF COLOURS AND HIT CHECK.
IF YOU RUN OUT OF TURNS THE GAME IS OVER.
STARTING FROM TOP TO BOTTOM, AT EACH ROW, CLICK IN THE CIRCLES AND PICK A COLOUR.


EVERY TIME YOU HIT CHECK YOU WILL GET FEEDBACK ON YOUR GUESS:
GREEN MEANS CORRECT COLOUR IN THE CORRECT POSITION.
ORANGE MEANS CORRECT COLOUR IN THE INCORRECT POSITION.
BLANK MEANS THE COLOUR YOU GUESSED IS NOT IN THE COMBO.
